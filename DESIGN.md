# Design System: EriuSports
**Project ID:** 11261012537877120353

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
EriuSports employs a hyper-commercial, premium D2C e-commerce aesthetic (inspired by modern frameworks like rocket.new). The design must compete with high-volume sports merchandise sites (like County Collectibles) but elevate the experience to feel more premium and striking.
* **Light Mode:** High-conversion, crisp, and high-energy. Features bold, massive typography, striking product cutouts on clean white canvases, asymmetric bento-box feature grids, and distinct social proof/urgency elements.
* **Dark Mode:** Edgy, high-octane sportswear vibe. Uses deep Midnight Navy backgrounds with glowing Atlantic Teal accents to make interactive elements unmissable.
The layout must prioritize shopping velocity: clear CTAs, prominent product grids, customer reviews, and sticky shopping components.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles (Light Mode)
* **Ériu Deep Emerald (#1A533E):** *Primary Brand & Accent.* A rich, dark, grounding green used for the main brand logo and primary call-to-action buttons.
* **Atlantic Teal (#1C7C83):** *Secondary Accent.* Vibrant teal used for active navigation states, progress indicators, and secondary buttons.
* **Midnight Navy (#0F2131):** *Primary Text & Strong Accents.* Extremely dark blue-black used for primary headings and heavy contrast elements.
* **Celtic White (#FFFFFF):** *Main Background.* The foundational bright, clean canvas.
* **Slate Grey (#404040):** *Standard Body Text.* Used for all body copy and standard descriptions.
* **Heather Grey (#757575):** *Secondary Text & Subtle Borders.* Used for subtitles, placeholders, divider lines.

## 3. Color Palette & Roles (Dark Mode)
* **Ériu Deep Emerald (#1A533E):** *Primary Brand & Subtle Background Adjustments.* Used strategically for dark, rich container backgrounds or subtle gradients. 
* **Atlantic Teal (#1C7C83):** *Primary Accent & Interactive Elements.* Acts as the high-visibility, energetic "pop" color on the dark background for buttons, links, and active states.
* **Midnight Navy (#0F2131):** *Main Background.* The foundational deep, moody canvas that makes interactive elements stand out.
* **Celtic White (#FFFFFF):** *Primary Text.* Used for all main headings and standard body copy to ensure maximum legibility against the dark background.
* **Heather Grey (#757575):** *Secondary Text & Prominent Borders.* Used for subtitles, muted elements, and visible but subtle borders.
* **Slate Grey (#404040):** *Surface Component Background (Elevated).* Used for cards, modals, and input backgrounds to create depth off the Midnight Navy base.

## 4. Typography Rules
* **Font Family:** **Lexend** (or a similar wide, modern, geometric sans-serif)
* **Logotype Style:** The main brand name "ÉRIUSPORTS" is displayed in wide, solid, uppercase lettering. 
* **Headers:** Employs medium to bold weights (500-700) with slight letter-spacing for high-impact headlines that convey technical precision.
* **Body Copy:** Uses regular weights (400) with comfortable line height for easy reading of product descriptions and details.
* **Labels / Accents / Taglines:** Uppercase styling with wide letter-spacing is used for overlines, directional labels, or the brand tagline ("DESIGNED IN IRELAND • BUILT FOR PERFORMANCE").

## 5. Component Stylings
* **Buttons:** 
  * *Shape:* Sharp, squared-off edges or very subtly rounded corners (`rounded-sm` or `rounded-none`).
  * *Style (Light):* Ériu Deep Emerald background with Celtic White text. Hover states shift to Atlantic Teal or Midnight Navy.
  * *Style (Dark):* Atlantic Teal or Celtic White background with Midnight Navy text for maximum pop. Hover states shift to Ériu Deep Emerald.
* **Cards/Containers:** 
  * *Shape:* Subtle rounded corners (4px) or sharp corners.
  * *Style (Light):* Celtic White backgrounds with delicate diffused shadows (e.g., rgba(15, 33, 49, 0.05) at 4px blur).
  * *Style (Dark):* Slate Grey backgrounds with soft, dark drop shadows or 1px Heather Grey borders.
* **Inputs/Forms:** 
  * *Shape:* Sharp or subtly rounded (4px) border-radius.
  * *Style (Light):* Bright white backgrounds with subtle Heather Grey borders. Atlantic Teal focus ring.
  * *Style (Dark):* Dark backgrounds (Midnight Navy or Slate Grey) with Heather Grey borders. Atlantic Teal focus ring.

## 6. Layout Principles (Premium D2C E-commerce)
* **Structure:** High-velocity conversion layouts. Use tight, asymmetric "bento-box" grids for feature highlights, and massive, unbroken full-width grids for product catalogs (similar to modern apparel drops).
* **Shopping UX:** Always include trust signals (star ratings, "Excellent 4.8/5" badges), urgency banners ("Selling Fast"), and highly visible sticky navigation/cart elements.
* **Contrast & Hierarchy:** Typography should be massive and unapologetic for heroes and section headers. CTAs (Ériu Deep Emerald or Atlantic Teal) must be the highest-contrast elements on the screen, instantly drawing the eye away from the Celtic White or Midnight Navy backgrounds.
